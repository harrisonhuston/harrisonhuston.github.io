Education

I graduated from the University of New Hampshire with a Bachelor of Science in 2014 and spent most of my career in the financial services industry. Looking for a change, I discovered software development/engineering. I began to teach myself front-end development and quickly fell in love with coding. With the hopes of pursuing a career in software development/engineering, I enrolled in Boston University's Master of Science in Software Development program, where I am a current student. Below is some more information regarding my studies thus far at Boston University.
